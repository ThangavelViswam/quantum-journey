# Chapter 1: Vectors — What even are they?

## 3 Perspectives
- **Physics**: arrow in space with direction and magnitude
- **CS**: ordered list of numbers e.g. [3, -2]
- **Math**: anything that can be added or scaled

## Key Concepts
- Vector tail always sits at the **origin** (0,0)
- Coordinates describe how far to move in x and y directions

## Two Fundamental Operations
- **Vector addition**: place tail of second vector at tip of first, draw to endpoint
- **Scalar multiplication**: stretches (>1), squishes (0-1), flips direction (<0)

## Why it matters for Quantum Computing
- Quantum states are represented as vectors
- These two operations (add + scale) are the foundation of ALL linear algebra
